Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

PhoneNav is an Android companion app for WristNav, providing route planning, GPX file management, and map downloading. This privacy policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

PhoneNav may collect and process the following data on your device:

  • Location data — GPS coordinates used for map display and route planning
  • GPX files — Route files you import or receive from your WristNav watch
  • Map files — Offline map files you download for use on your watch

How We Use Your Data

All data collected by PhoneNav is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal data to any external servers operated by us.

  • Location data is used solely to display your position on the map and assist with route planning
  • GPX files are stored on your device and only shared when you explicitly choose to send them to your watch or export them
  • Map files are downloaded from OpenStreetMap-based servers and stored locally on your device

Data Sharing

PhoneNav does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When you explicitly send a GPX file or map to your WristNav watch
  • When you explicitly share a GPX file using your device's share functionality

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. GPX files and map files are saved to your device's storage. You can delete any data at any time by removing files from your device or uninstalling the app.

Permissions

PhoneNav requests the following permissions:

  • Location — for displaying your position on the map and route planning
  • Storage — to save and read GPX and map files
  • Internet — to download map tiles and map files
  • Nearby devices / Bluetooth — to communicate with your WristNav watch

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
