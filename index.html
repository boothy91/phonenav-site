Features

🌍

Fully Offline Maps

Download OpenStreetMap regions to your device. Navigate anywhere with no data connection needed.

📍

GPX Route Navigation

Import or plan GPX routes and follow them on the map with live position tracking.

📊

Activity Recording

Track distance, pace, heart rate, steps, elevation gain and loss during your activity.

🗂️

Route Planner

Plan routes directly in the app and save them as GPX files ready to navigate.

🎨

Themes

Light, Dark, and Fjord themes to suit any environment or preference.

⌚

WristNav Companion

Pair with WristNav on your Wear OS watch for wrist-based navigation on the trail.

How to Install

1

Download the APK from the button above

2

Enable Install from unknown sources in Android Settings

3

Open the downloaded APK file and tap Install
